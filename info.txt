Main Packet Types

gaa (multiple times) — Map Area Updates
Shows all nearby castles, outposts, and players around your location.
Lots of data about alliances, levels, troops on field, etc.

gie — Guild / Alliance info update
sce — Score / Statistic update
cat — Attack Confirmation (very important)
sne — System notification / chat message
dms — Delete March / March finished
gxp — Player XP / Level update



look out for a cat associated with an attack after sent
